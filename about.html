Get to know more about Jaya

Journey towards Therapist

Jaya Banala is a dedicated expert for personal empowerment and mindset transformation. With years of experience in personal development and wellness, has devoted their career to helping others unlock their potential and live a life of abundance. As a sought-after speaker and workshop leader, Jaya has inspired countless individuals to shift their perspectives and embrace the possibilities that life offers. When not writing or speaking, Jaya enjoys meditating and researching of innovative ways to fuel their commitment to living a fulfilling and balanced life.

Jaya believes that everyone has the power to create the life they desire and is ready to help you on your journey toward a more prosperous and joyful soul.

Jaya's unique approach combines scientific principles with holistic practices, making self-love accessible and achievable for everyone. Through workshops, coaching sessions, and now this comprehensive guide, they have empowered countless individuals to embrace their authentic selves and lead happier, more fulfilling lives.

Jaya believes in the power of gratitude, mindfulness, and self-compassion. They live in Birmingham, UK with very supportive husband and most beautiful daughter and very loving Zeke(their pet dog).

Her spiritual journey began when she moved to the UK alone, leaving her four-year-old child and husband behind in pursuit of a brighter future for her family. She has been associated with various faiths such as Brahma Kumaris, ISKCON, and the Isha Foundation. Ultimately, she discovered and experienced God as a form of energy, realizing that traditional religion can sometimes restrict our ability to fully connect with this immense source of power.
